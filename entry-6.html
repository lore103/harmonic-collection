⚲ New York City

Snowstorm

This photo was taken on a day when the first snowstorm in New York took place in 2022. We all went to our friend’s place in Bushwick and had a sleepover there. It was really fun because during the night we watched ‘The Parent Trap’ and drank hot chocolate. When we woke up and we saw the streets covered in snow. This was so fascinating to me because it never snows in Hong Kong. We made pancakes for breakfast and then we went to the park nearby to make snow angels and snowmen. I think that was definitely one of my favourite days, especially since I was going through a rough couple of days previously. One thing that I’ve learnt about the snow days in New York is that it’s great for the first 30 minutes and then after that I wish for summer again.
