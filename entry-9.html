hotel flicks

I took these photos while quarantining in Hong Kong for a week. After watching so many movies and finishing the second season of Euphoria I was experimenting with the different makeup looks from the show. After trying out a specific eye look, I decided to take some photos with my film camera since I was really bored. I used the self-timer effect on it and got inspiration from Pinterest of different hotel room pictures. From those photos I tried to pick out a more classic and basic outfit, which was a bit more difficult because I didn’t have as many sophisticated pieces with me. It was fun but it was definitely a struggle trying to balance the camera on different objects since I didn’t have a tripod. The pictures definitely turned out really nice which I’m extremely happy about. I used Adobe Lightroom to adjust the size and colours of the photo.
