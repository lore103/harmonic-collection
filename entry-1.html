"Prom"

This photo was taken back in August 2020 in Hong Kong. This was one of the last times where my high school friends and I were all together in the same place and same time. Because of the pandemic, we weren’t even able to have an actual graduation prom. Instead, we booked a venue and took pictures instead with our dresses. Of course, prom isn’t the same without the actual event. However, my heart felt so full after this.
