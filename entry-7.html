⚲ Hong Kong

Snowstorm

This photo was taken when I went to HKMOA (Hong Kong Museum of Art) with my friend. We were supposed to go to an art and design exhibition at a university in Hong Kong (The Hong Kong Polytechnic University) but they didn’t allow walk-in visits from outside visitors. Therefore we ended up at the museum. There were a lot of cool exhibits. I went there recently with another friend but we didn’t thoroughly look at the exhibits so being able to this time was really satisfying. The picture was taken with tungsten film so the effect turned out really nice. I also didn’t use flash for the picture so the colour from the screen looks really vibrant. The colours also look as if they’re glowing which adds a lot to the photograph.
